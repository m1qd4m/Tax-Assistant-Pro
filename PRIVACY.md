# Privacy Policy for Tax Helper Pakistan

**Last Updated:** June 13, 2026

Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy details how **Tax Helper** ("we", "our", or "the app") handles data.

---

### 1. No Data Collection (Offline-First)
Tax Helper is an **offline-first** utility. All calculations, wizard steps, and inputs are processed locally on your mobile device. 
* We do **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal, financial, or tax-related information.
* There is no database, no remote server connectivity, and no user registration required.

---

### 2. Third-Party Services & Analytics
* We do **not** use any third-party advertising networks, analytical trackers, or user profiling SDKs inside the app.
* No cookies, location trackers, or background telemetry are utilized.

---

### 3. Device Permissions
The app runs standard Expo/React Native wrappers and does not request or require permission to access sensitive device services such as:
* Contacts
* Device Location
* Camera or Photos
* Device storage (write/read)

---

### 4. Open Source Transparency
Tax Helper is an open-source project designed for portfolio demonstration purposes. You can inspect the complete source code, libraries, and compilation configurations at our public repository:
[https://github.com/Miqdaam/Tax-Assistant-Pro](https://github.com/Miqdaam/Tax-Assistant-Pro)

---

### 5. Contact Information
If you have any questions or concerns regarding this policy, please reach out to us by opening an issue on our GitHub repository.
